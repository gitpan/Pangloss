Pangloss

Welcome to Pangloss, a multilingual glossary.
  • about
    Pangloss is a terminology management system who's goal is to help translators produce consistent translations of common terms across multiple languages. The system allows users to search through and manage terms and their translations.

  • search
    You can search through terms by going to the search page.

  • login
    You can login to the system by going to the login page. While Pangloss is in development you can choose to login as any user listed under the users tab.
